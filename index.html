氢气经济模具交互界面 / Hydrogen Economy Model Interactive Interface

把 Excel 中的关键制氢参数转成可调输入，实时查看人民币单位制氢成本、美元折算、年产量、NPV 和投资回收期。 / Converts key Excel hydrogen parameters into adjustable inputs and shows RMB LCOH, USD conversion, annual output, NPV and payback.

输入参数 / Inputs

Excel: simu-H2 Price
设备价采用 2025-2026 中国市场单 kW 口径正常成交带：ALK 1200-1600 元/kW、PEM 2800-3200 元/kW（含电源/BOP；低于成本的极端中标价如 ALK 416、PEM 1490 元/kW 不作基准）。项目规模测算采用 1000 Nm3/h = 5 MW 简化口径。折现率基准：央企新能源税前全投资 IRR 红线 6%-6.5%（大唐 2025），本模型默认 5% 仅作筛选。 / Equipment prices use the 2025-26 China sustainable band on an RMB/kW basis: ALK 1,200-1,600, PEM 2,800-3,200 (incl. rectifier/BOP; below-cost outliers such as ALK 416 and PEM 1,490 RMB/kW excluded). SOE hurdle-rate benchmark: 6-6.5% pre-tax full-investment IRR (Datang 2025); the 5% default is for screening only.

绿电到原料价格 / Renewable Power to Product Prices

H2 -> NH3 / MeOH / eSAF
原料价格 = 绿电价驱动的制氢成本 × 单吨产品耗氢 + CO2 成本 × 单吨产品耗 CO2 + 辅助电耗 × 绿电价 + 其他转化成本。默认值用于战略筛选，可按具体装置、碳源、认证边界和副产物分摊调整。 / Product price = power-linked H2 cost x H2 consumption + CO2 cost x CO2 consumption + auxiliary power x renewable power price + other conversion cost. Defaults are for screening and can be adjusted for the actual plant, carbon source, certification boundary and by-product allocation.
